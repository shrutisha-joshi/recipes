Ingredients:
-2 packets Parle-G
-1 packet oreo chocolate
-Milk
-1 Packet ENO

Directions:
1.Grind all the contents above in mixxy and then make a batter using milk.
2.Preheat the oven.
3.Before putting batter into bowl,grease it with oil.
4.Add eno into the batter,mix it and put it in bowl and microwave for 5 min.The cake is ready!
